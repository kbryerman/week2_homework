DESIGN + DEVELOP

San Francisco based designer/developer with a passion for building beautiful things.

EXPLORE

It all started visually. I’m not sure if the path was linear, but the obsession was there. I was the kid in the corner folding pieces of paper into different origami patterns and the one building, modeling, exploring with different forms of media and technology; I was fascinated by how beautifully the pieces came together. Naturally, I dove deep into the architectural world throughout high school and college and set out to San Francisco with foggy dreams of parametric data and it’s influence on design. Now, three years later and unsatisfied with my architectural experiences, my vision has begun to shift.
